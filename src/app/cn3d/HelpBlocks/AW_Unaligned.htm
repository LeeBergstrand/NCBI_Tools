Sequence Window : Unaligned Justification Commands

The horizontal position of unaligned (lower-case) residues is arbitrary, and this menu determines how the unaligned residues are distributed in the spaces between aligned blocks. The options are:

 

  • Left: unaligned residues are grouped on the left side of the available space.
  • Right: unaligned residues are grouped on the right side of the available space.
  • Center: unaligned residues are grouped together in the center of the available space.
  • Split: unaligned residues are grouped half on the left side and half on the right side of the available space.
